# AmbientRide — Account & Data Deletion

## Request Account Deletion

To delete your AmbientRide account and all associated data:

### Option 1: In the App
1. Open AmbientRide
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon on Home screen)
3. Scroll to the bottom
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm the deletion

### Option 2: Via Email
Send an email to **privacy@ambientride.app** with:
- Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
- Your registered email address

We will process your request within 7 business days.

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your user profile (name, email, photo)
- Vehicle details
- All ride requests (pending, matched, completed)
- All match records
- Driver location data
- CO₂ savings ledger
- Chat messages
- Ratings given and received
- FCM push notification token
- Local AI model data (on-device)
- Federated learning contributions (gradient updates)

## What Is NOT Deleted

- Anonymized, aggregated statistics (e.g., total platform CO₂ savings)
- Data required for legal compliance (retained for up to 30 days after deletion)

## Data Portability

Before deleting your account, you can export your data from the app:
- CO₂ Impact screen shows your complete ride history
- Profile screen shows your personal information
